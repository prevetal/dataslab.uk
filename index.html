Our Tech Stack

Dataslab's experienced team is proficient in developing and implementing on-site solutions, from bespoke hardware specifications to client requirements to hardware acquisition, setup, and configuration. Likewise, cloud-based solutions are also built in collaboration with reputable providers such as AWS or Microsoft Azure.

Our expertise spans a wide range of technologies, including licensed software products like Cloudera Data Platform, Cloudera Machine Learning, Microsoft Power BI, and Tibco Data Virtualization.

We also utilise open-source software such as Apache Hadoop, Apache Nifi, and Apache Kafka, which are heavily used in the Dataslab Data Platform, ensuring we efficiently architect Big Data, IoT, and AI/ML solutions to meet our clients' needs.
